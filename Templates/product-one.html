Temple Description

Koothanur Maha Saraswathi Temple is a Hindu temple located in the town of Koothanur in the Tiruvarur district of Tamil Nadu, India. It is dedicated to Saraswathi, the Hindu goddess of learning. Worshipping or offering Pujas in this temple will help people to excel in their studies. White lotus flower is the preferred offering to the deity.
