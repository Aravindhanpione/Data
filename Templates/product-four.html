Temple Description

Rajagopalaswamy temple is a Vaishnavite shrine located in the town of Mannargudi, Tamil Nadu, India. The temple is called Dakshina Dwaraka (Southern Dwaraka) along with Guruvayoor by Hindus. The presiding deity is Rajagopalaswamy, a form of Lord Krishna. In this temple, lord Sri Krishna appears as a shepherd boy with a cow and two calves by his side. A puja at this temple is considered to be very powerful and helps parents who do not have children. Childless couples need to perform Ghee Prasad Puja to conceive.
